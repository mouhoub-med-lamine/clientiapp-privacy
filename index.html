Privacy Policy for ClientiApp

Last updated: February 13, 2026

ClientiApp ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

Local Data Storage

ClientiApp works primarily offline. All client data, follow-up notes, and schedules you enter are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit this data to our servers.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • App usage data

This information is used to provide and improve advertising. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings.

For more information, please see:
Google Privacy Policy

Notifications

The app may request permission to send push notifications for follow-up reminders. These notifications are handled locally and may use Expo or Firebase services, which may process technical data necessary for message delivery.

2. Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. The only third-party data processing occurs through Google AdMob for advertising purposes as described above.

3. Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. We recommend enabling a device passcode or biometric lock to protect your information.

4. Children’s Privacy

ClientiApp is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Your Choices

You can disable personalized ads in your device settings. You can also uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
